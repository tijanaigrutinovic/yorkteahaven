Tea in the UK

Tea in the UK is more than just a beverage – it’s a ritual! From the iconic ‘afternoon tea’ to the everyday cup, tea is deeply embedded in British culture. Whether it's for socializing or taking a break, Brits are known to drink tea at any time of the day.

Did you know?

Britons drink an average of 100 million cups of tea every day!

How to Brew the Perfect Tea?

Water Temperature

For black tea, water should be nearly boiling (around 100°C), while for green tea, aim for around 70-80°C.

The right water temperature is essential for brewing your tea perfectly. Boiling water extracts full flavor from black tea, while cooler water helps preserve the delicate taste of green tea.

Brew Time

For black tea, brew for 3-5 minutes. Green tea only needs 2-3 minutes.

The brew time determines the strength of your tea. For a perfect cup, avoid steeping for too long as it can make the tea bitter.
